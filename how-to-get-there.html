How to Get to Siquijor

Your comprehensive guide to reaching our mystical island paradise

Transportation Options

Choose your preferred way to reach Siquijor Island and begin your magical journey.

By Air

Direct flights to Dumaguete Airport (DGT) or Tagbilaran Airport (TAG). Daily flights available from Manila and Cebu.

  • Flight Duration: 1-1.5 hours
  • Multiple airlines available
  • Year-round service

By Ferry

Regular ferry services from Dumaguete, Cebu, and Bohol ports. Fast craft and slow ferry options available.

  • Multiple daily trips
  • Various vessel types
  • Affordable rates

Travel Tips

Essential information for a smooth journey to Siquijor Island.

  • Book tickets in advance
  • Check weather conditions
  • Pack travel essentials

Schedule

Ferry departure times and booking information.

  • First trip: 5:30 AM
